FocusLoom Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 7, 2025

FocusLoom is designed to be a local-first productivity dashboard. We do not run a backend or collect analytics. This page explains what data is stored and how it is used.

Data Stored Locally

  • Your name, selected timezone, and preferred text color.
  • Widget preferences (weather/todo/pomodoro visibility, search behavior).
  • Todo items and Pomodoro state.
  • Weather city and your personal OpenWeather API key (if you provide one).

All of the items above are saved inside your browser's localStorage. Nothing is synced or transmitted unless you manually export it.

Network Requests

  • OpenWeather: fetching weather data requires an API key that you provide. Requests are sent directly to api.openweathermap.org.
  • Search suggestions: FocusLoom requests completions from duckduckgo.com to populate the search dropdown.

Third Parties

No other third parties receive data from FocusLoom. We do not embed analytics, advertising trackers, or social widgets.

Your Choices

  • You can clear all FocusLoom data at any time by clearing browser storage for the extension.
  • You may choose not to enter an OpenWeather API key. In that case, the weather widget remains disabled.
